## What is FloraFleet?

FloraFleet keeps an eye on your plants so you don't have to. Sensor nodes
across your greenhouse or growing site report conditions continuously, and
everything is managed from one simple dashboard — setup, monitoring, and
updates included.

## Why FloraFleet?

- **🌡️ Always watching** — continuous readings from every corner of your site, in one place.
- **📶 Works anywhere** — nodes stay connected across large sites without relying on existing WiFi.
- **🔌 Plug & grow** — new sensors join in minutes, no technical setup required.
- **🔄 Always current** — devices keep themselves up to date automatically.
- **📊 One dashboard** — see your whole fleet at a glance, from any browser.
